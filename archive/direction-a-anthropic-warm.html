Kyle DS Direction A: Anthropic Warm

Direction Overview

Base: Warm cream backgrounds inspired by Anthropic's institutional warmth. Not cool, not sterile.
Accent: Warm orange/terracotta used surgically for interactive elements and emphasis only.
Typography: Instrument Serif for editorial headings, Inter for systematic body text, JetBrains Mono for code.
Approach: Hierarchy through type weight and size. Borders over shadows. Generous whitespace.
Colors: All tokens defined in oklch(). Three-tier architecture: primitives, semantic, component.
Dark mode: Intentionally designed warm dark — not inverted. Accent lightened, borders softened.

Token Architecture
