01 — Philosophy

Warm clarity, surgical color, joyful restraint

Delightful draws from institutional minimalism and developer-tool precision. Every decision serves comprehension. Color is used surgically. Motion earns its place.

Warm Clarity
Cream backgrounds, near-black text, generous whitespace. Typography does the heavy lifting — hierarchy through size, weight, and spacing, never through ornament.
Anthropic · Editorial restraint
Surgical Color
One vivid accent for actions. Red for danger. Yellow for highlights. Green for success. That's it. Color is earned, never decorative.
Linear · Maximum restraint
Joyful Restraint
Playful but controlled. Spring-based motion under 240ms. Border separation over shadows. Every interaction should feel inevitable, not performative.
Arc · Controlled personality
What we do
Warm offwhite base
Not pure white, not cool. Cream-tinted backgrounds like Anthropic's interface.
Typography-driven hierarchy
Size, weight, spacing — not color or decoration — create visual order.
Borders over shadows
Shadows reserved for overlays only. Borders and color differences create depth.
Motion with purpose
Sub-240ms springs for movement. CSS ease-out for color. prefers-reduced-motion respected.
What we avoid
Gradients & glassmorphism
No frosted glass aesthetics, no gradient backgrounds, no heavy decoration.
Rainbow palettes
No overly colorful systems. Four purposeful accent families, applied surgically.
Generic SaaS energy
No cookie-cutter templates. Every interface should feel crafted for its context.
Placeholder text
Real content always. No lorem ipsum. Words carry meaning and inform design.

Motion Guidelines
Use ease-out for...
Color changes, opacity fades, border transitions. Anything that responds immediately and decelerates naturally.
100–160ms · CSS transitions
Use ease-bounce for...
Toggle switches, checkboxes, small interactive elements. Creates playful overshooting that adds personality.
160–240ms · Spring-like feel
Use ease-smooth for...
Entrance animations, page transitions, modal reveals. Smooth deceleration creates a natural, controlled feel.
240–360ms · Entrance/exit
Never animate...
Layout shifts that cause content reflow, font-size changes, or anything that blocks user interaction. Keep the thread clear.
Avoid transform + layout simultaneously
