H3 — step-2 / 600 / -0.02em / leading 1.15

Primary colors, not primary school

H4 — step-1 / 600 / -0.02em / leading 1.3

Every interaction tells a story

Body — step-0 / 400 / leading 1.55

The best design systems do more than enforce consistency — they encode a point of view. When every button press has a satisfying bounce, when colors feel confident rather than cautious, when the spacing breathes without wasting space, you build something people genuinely enjoy using. Joy in tools is not frivolous. It reduces friction, increases engagement, and turns daily workflows into moments of quiet satisfaction. Red, blue, and yellow are not childish. They are the foundation of every visual system humans have ever built, from Mondrian to Google to the flags of nations. Used with precision and restraint, primary colors communicate confidence that no muted palette can match.
